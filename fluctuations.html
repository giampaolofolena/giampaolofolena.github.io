Heterogeneous Fluctuations and Rare Trajectories in Mean-Field Models

Heterogeneous fluctuations reveal how microscopic rearrangements appear inside coarse mean-field descriptions of glasses. This work combines replica calculations and numerical simulations to characterize non-Gaussian fluctuations near the glass transition, with an emphasis on rare trajectories and their relation to single-particle correlations.
